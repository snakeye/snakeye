# Andrey Ovcharov

Independent systems builder exploring structured and verifiable AI.

For more than 25 years I’ve been building production systems. Not pitch decks. Not “AI strategies”. Real things: backend systems, distributed services, hardware devices, internal tools, public services. Some small. Some are used by millions. Most of them were boring in the best possible way — they worked.

Over time, one theme kept repeating: I don’t like ambiguity in systems. I prefer explicit models over magic. I prefer inspectable structure over opaque heuristics. And I like owning the whole loop — from idea to something that actually runs.

Right now, my main focus is structured AI: how to put a clear, inspectable knowledge layer on top of large language models instead of pretending that probability is reasoning.

---

## Current Focus

### Cogentis AI

* https://cogentis-ai.com  
* https://github.com/cogentis-ai  

LLMs are impressive, but they don’t *know* anything. They generate plausible text. Cogentis is my attempt to build a structured knowledge layer around them: concept graphs, explicit relationships, inference rules, verifiable reasoning.

Status: active research prototype.  
Focus: knowledge modelling, graph structures, reasoning pipelines.

This is not an “AI wrapper”. It’s an attempt to make systems that can be reasoned about.

## Selected Projects

### Exploristo  

* https://exploristo.com  
* https://github.com/exploristo  

An experimental semantic explorer for places and localities.

Most travel tools are flat lists with filters. Exploristo treats places as a graph: relationships, context, and locality structure. It’s an applied knowledge graph in a concrete domain.

Status: experimental platform.  

Focus: graph modelling, spatial data, and semantic relationships.

### TelePico  

* https://telepico.com  
* https://github.com/telepico  

A minimal service for publishing images on the open web.

No feed. No algorithm decides whether your content deserves to exist. You upload an image and get a public page with a stable URL. It started as a Telegram bot and grew into a simple publishing pipeline.

Status: active tool.  

Focus: minimal infrastructure, Telegram integration, public web delivery.

### Dear Hiring Team  

* https://dearhiring.team  
* https://github.com/dearhiringteam  

A structured job application tracking system.

Hiring is usually opaque and chaotic. DearHiring treats it as a system: states, transitions, history, explicit pipeline. No magic. No “maybe they’ll reply”.

## How I Think About Engineering

I distrust black boxes.

I prefer systems that can be inspected, reasoned about, and debugged without superstition.

I don’t get excited about hype cycles. I get interested when a model is explicit and constraints are clear.

I’m comfortable working across layers — backend, infrastructure, data, sometimes frontend — because real systems don’t respect artificial boundaries.

If something is worth building, it’s worth building properly.
